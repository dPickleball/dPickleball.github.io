About

dPickleball empowers university and college students from Malaysia to embrace problem-solving, compete for excellence, and ignite their passion for programming, AI, data science, and biomedical innovation through technology-driven competition. We connect bright minds and cultivate the next generation of tech talent, shaping the future of innovation-all while competing for a prestigious championship trophy and a total cash prize of up to RM10,000 üèÜ With three thrilling categories:

(A) AI vs. AI

Participants develop AI-driven agents to compete in digital pickleball matches. Teams design and train machine learning models, reinforcement learning agents, or rule-based algorithms to strategize, predict movements, and outperform opposing AIs in a fully simulated environment. This challenges students to push the limits of AI strategy, optimization, and real-time decision-making.

(B) HCI vs. HCI

This category focuses on Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) by using motion tracking, pose estimation, and biomechanics to control gameplay. Competitors develop systems that analyze human body movements captured via camera-based pose estimation or motion sensors to interact with the game. The goal is to create the most accurate, responsive, and intuitive pickleball-playing system.

(C) BCI vs. BCI

The most futuristic category, Brain-Computer Interface (BCI), challenges students to control the game using neural signals (EEG). Competitors design brain-controlled interfaces that translate thoughts or mental commands into in-game actions. This pushes the boundaries of neuroscience, AI, and real-time signal processing, creating an innovative space where mindpower meets competition.
